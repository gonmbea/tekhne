I’m a Computer Science student at Dalhousie University with an interest in data analysis, machine learning, and the places where technology overlaps with art. You can read more about me and my past work. Most of my personal code is on GitHub.

Why Tekhne?

In Greek, tekhne means art. Since "technology" comes from that root, I thought it was a fitting name for my portfolio.

For me, exploring the intersection of art and technology feels natural, sometimes through code, other times through more hands on work like sketching or crochet. Both let me play with structure, randomness, and color.

About Me

I’ve worked on projects that connect technology with creativity and data-driven insights. I believe beauty and art has everything to do with math and science and I like to explore projects where those fields overlap. My work blends software engineering practices with a curiosity for research and user experience.
